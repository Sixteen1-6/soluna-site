Soluna started with a simple idea: the game you play for fun still deserves things made with care. We build for the player who shows up because they love it, not because of a ranking.

For the love of it

Made for the player who plays for love, not ranking.

Pickleball is the highlight of a lot of people's week. The morning game, the standing match with friends, the long sunny weekends on the court. We think that hobby is worth taking seriously, even if the score never matters much.

So we make things you are proud to carry. A paddle worth two or three hundred dollars deserves better than a plastic sleeve, and a good summer deserves a tee you actually reach for. Quiet, considered, built to last more than a season.

Made in Lexington

Kentucky roots, sunny days

Soluna is Blake and Shub, two friends from Lexington, Kentucky. The brand grew out of the way we already spent our summers: outside, on the court, chasing the part of the day when the light is best and nobody is keeping a real score.

We start with a small first batch, made close to home, in colorways we actually wanted to own. No rush, no noise. Just a few good things, released when they are ready.

Everything we make is made for sunny days. The structured Case in Blush and Marina. A heavyweight cream tee. A low navy cap with a small embroidered S. Pieces that look as good off the court as on it.

If that sounds like your kind of game, you are exactly who we built this for. The first drop is coming soon, and we would love to have you there for it.
