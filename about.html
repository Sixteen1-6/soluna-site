For the love of it

A hobby that good is worth taking seriously.

Pickleball is the best part of a lot of people's week. The morning game. The standing match with friends. The long, sunny weekends that run until the light goes. We think a hobby that good is worth taking seriously, even when the score never really mattered.

So we make things you are proud to carry. A good paddle deserves better than a plastic sleeve, and a good summer deserves a tee you actually reach for. Considered, and built to last more than a season.

Our Story

How Soluna started

Soluna started with an injury.

Blake was on his way to play college baseball when a boating accident wrecked his knee and ended that plan. Pickleball was one of the first things he could play again. It gave the competition back.

But the gear never matched how good the game felt. Every bag, ball and shirt on the shelf was plastic and forgettable, nothing you would be proud to carry. We thought the people who love this game deserved better, so we started making it ourselves.
